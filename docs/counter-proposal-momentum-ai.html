Content Corrections Required

Non-negotiable: A platform built on trust cannot launch with content that undermines that trust. The following must be corrected in all materials:

  • No fabricated testimonials. The "Real Community Impact" quotes in the Civic Leaders portal describe scenarios that have not happened. Replace with verifiable statements or remove entirely. Use aspirational framing ("Here's how organizations can use Quarex") instead of fake quotes.
  • Accurate statistics only. "10,000+ Local races indexed" is not true. Use real numbers: 641 books, 3,205 chapters, 435 House races, 33 Senate races, 36 Governor races, all 50 states covered. These are strong numbers. They don't need inflation.
  • No zip code lookup promise. The "Enter your location, see your personalized ballot" feature does not currently exist. Describe what Quarex actually offers: structured state-by-state navigation to every federal race. That's already more than any competitor provides.
