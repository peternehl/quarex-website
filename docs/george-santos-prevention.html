The Questions That Would Have Caught Santos

When you select a candidate on Quarex, it automatically generates questions like:

"What false or misleading public claims has George Santos made in the last 30 days?"
"Does George Santos have a criminal record, history of bankruptcy, or any other significant legal issues?"
"What conflicts of interest exist between George Santos's public role and their financial, business, or personal ties?"

These aren't gotcha questions. They're the basic due diligence every voter should be able to do - but rarely has time to formulate on their own.
