Deliverables

Project work, campaign proposals, and creative output.

This section will be populated as project teams submit their work. Deliverables may include marketing strategies, campaign materials, social media plans, guerrilla marketing proposals, and market research.

Expected Deliverables

  • Marketing Strategy — Comprehensive plan for increasing Quarex awareness and adoption
  • Audience Research — Surveys, interviews, or analysis of target audience behavior and preferences
  • Campaign Concepts — Creative campaigns with messaging, visuals, and distribution channels
  • Social Media Plan — Platform-specific content calendar and engagement strategy
  • Guerrilla Marketing — Low-budget, high-impact tactics for grassroots awareness
  • Metrics and KPIs — How to measure success and track campaign performance

Submission Guidelines

  • All deliverables should reference the brand guidelines for consistent presentation
  • Campaigns should target one or more of the defined audiences
  • Include rationale for creative decisions, not just the output
  • Consider the competitive landscape when positioning Quarex
