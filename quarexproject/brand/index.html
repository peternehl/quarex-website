Brand Guidelines

Consistent brand presentation across all Quarex marketing materials.

Brand Identity

Quarex positions itself as a serious, trustworthy knowledge tool. The brand voice is confident but not flashy, educational but not condescending, and honest above all else.

Tagline

"A curiosity engine for exploring complex ideas"

One-Sentence Description

Quarex is a structured, question-driven knowledge system that uses layered context to help people explore and understand ideas, rather than just search for information.

Typography

  • Headlines: Playfair Display (serif, bold) — conveys authority and timelessness
  • Body text: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable, modern
  • Fallback stack: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, sans-serif

Voice and Tone

  • Honest. Quarex exists to deliver truth. Never oversell, never spin.
  • Clear. Explain complex ideas simply. If someone can't understand it, rewrite it.
  • Confident but humble. Quarex is powerful, but it's the questions that matter, not the tool.
  • Non-partisan. Quarex serves everyone regardless of political affiliation.
  • Educational, not condescending. Respect the reader's intelligence while making things accessible.
