A Note on Stub Books

Some books in the newer curricula are "framework books" — they have chapter titles but the detailed topic questions haven't been filled in yet. These still work: when you open a chapter on Quarex, the AI uses the chapter title and context to infer relevant questions. So asking generic questions like "What should I know about this?" or "Explain the key ideas" will still produce useful answers.

Over time, we'll fill in the detailed topic lists. But don't let an empty topic array stop you from exploring.
