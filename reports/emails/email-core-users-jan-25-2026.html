Quarex Update: New Geography Library + Zero-Sum Politics

Two significant additions to Quarex this week:

New Library: Territories and Dependencies

Not every place in the world fits neatly into the "country" box. Hong Kong isn't China. Greenland isn't Denmark. Taiwan is... complicated. Most geography resources either ignore these places or misclassify them.

Quarex now has a dedicated Territories and Dependencies library covering 19 places across four categories:

Autonomous Regions: Åland Islands, Faroe Islands, Greenland, Hong Kong, Macau, Puerto Rico

Overseas Territories: Bermuda, Falkland Islands, French Polynesia, Gibraltar, Guam

Disputed Territories: Kosovo, Palestine, Taiwan, Western Sahara

Constituent Nations: Catalonia, Quebec, Scotland, Wales

Each entry covers:

  • National Overview - Status, population, economy, language
  • History - How they became what they are
  • Government and Laws - Powers, relationship to parent state
  • Travel Guidance - Entry requirements, attractions, logistics

Timely additions given Trump's renewed interest in Greenland and ongoing tensions around Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Palestine.

New Book: Zero-Sum Politics
