Interactive Maps: Now for Every Country, State, and Territory

The interactive map feature has been expanded massively. When reading about any location in the Geography Libraries, you can now click "View Map" to see an interactive OpenStreetMap view of that place.

New coverage includes:

  • Every country in the world — 195 nations with accurate coordinates and zoom levels
  • All 50 US states + DC — from Alabama to Wyoming
  • US territories — Puerto Rico, Guam, American Samoa, US Virgin Islands, Northern Mariana Islands
  • Global territories and dependencies — Greenland, Hong Kong, Scotland, Quebec, and more

New feature: Local/English toggle — Switch between map labels in the local language (using OpenStreetMap) or English (using CartoDB Voyager). Want to see Tokyo written as 東京 or as "Tokyo"? Now you can choose.

Read about a country, then explore its geography on the map in a separate view. The back button returns you exactly where you were.
