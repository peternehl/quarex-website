Quarex Update: Two New Sites Join the Platform

Quarex started as one site — a curiosity engine built from structured questions and contextual discovery. This week it became a family of four, each one designed around a different audience and a different way in.

PublicStudies.org — Curated Curricula

The idea behind PublicStudies is simple: take the books already inside Quarex and arrange them into reading paths that follow the structure of a real university discipline.

A curriculum on PublicStudies doesn't duplicate content. It points to it. Each course is a link into a specific Quarex book, and each book lives on its shelf alongside related books that provide natural context. The curriculum gives you the sequence; Quarex gives you the depth.

Live now:

  • Women's Studies — 3 courses including feminist theory, women's rights history, and Eleanor Roosevelt's biography
  • Black Studies — 2 courses covering the history of racism in the Americas and the Civil Rights Movement
  • Ethnic Studies — 1 course on the history of racism in the Americas

Coming soon: Labor Studies, Estudios Latinos, and Estudios de la Mujer.

Notice that the same book can appear in multiple curricula. A History of Racism in the Americas shows up in both Black Studies and Ethnic Studies, because it's relevant to both. The book doesn't move. The curricula are pointers, not containers.

This is the same model a university uses: one textbook, many syllabi. The difference is that here, the textbook is a living Quarex book that connects to every other book on its shelf.

Visit: publicstudies.org

QuarexProject.org — The Marketing Hub

As Quarex grows, it becomes harder to explain what the platform is in a single conversation. QuarexProject.org exists to solve that problem.

It's a brand and marketing site — not for end users, but for people who need to understand what Quarex is, how it works, and why it matters. Potential partners, marketers, educators, journalists, and anyone evaluating the platform can find what they need in one place.

What's there now:

  • Brand identity, voice, and design principles
  • The Election 2026 marketing brief with target audience data
  • Examples of past email campaigns
  • Competitive landscape analysis
  • Links to all live Quarex properties

Visit: quarexproject.org
