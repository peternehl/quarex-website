Quarex Update: New Visual Navigation

This week's focus: making the platform easier to explore. Two new visual tools let you see the entire Quarex ecosystem at a glance and navigate anywhere with a click.

Quarex Library Navigator

The Library Navigator is an interactive map of the entire Quarex taxonomy — all 7 library types, every library, and every shelf, displayed as a clickable visual hierarchy.

What you can do:

  • See the full structure — 825 books organized across Event, Geography, Knowledge, Infrastructure, Perspectives, Politicians, and Practical libraries
  • Zoom and pan — scroll to zoom, drag to explore different areas
  • Click any node — jump directly to that library type, library, or shelf
  • Understand the taxonomy — see how topics relate to each other at a glance

This solves a real problem: with 800+ books across dozens of shelves, it's easy to miss what's available. The Navigator shows you everything, organized visually.

Try it: Quarex Library Navigator

Ecosystem Navigator

The Ecosystem Navigator shows all the Quarex properties in one view — the central platform and all the satellite sites that connect to it.

The ecosystem includes:

  • Libraries — 825 Living Books, the core of everything
  • Public Studies — Curated curricula at publicstudies.org
  • Blog — Commentary and updates
  • Knowable Online — The university portal
  • Election 2026 — Every Congressional race
  • Elecciones 2026 — Spanish-language election coverage

Click any node to navigate there instantly. It's a simple visual that answers the question: "What is Quarex, exactly?"

Try it: Quarex Ecosystem Navigator

New Book: The US Intelligence Community

Why Navigation Matters

A platform with 825 books isn't useful if people can't find what they're looking for. These tools make discovery effortless:

  • The Library Navigator shows you the depth — every shelf, every topic area
  • The Ecosystem Navigator shows you the breadth — every Quarex property, one click away

Both load instantly and work on any device. Small files, fast performance.
