About Election 2026

Election2026.net is a free, nonpartisan site that lets any voter in the United States research their 2026 candidates and races. It is built entirely on Quarex.

What It Does

Enter your zip code and immediately find your US House, Senate, and Governor races with links to in-depth, AI-powered candidate profiles. Every profile is a Quarex book — structured, sourced, and free of editorial spin.

The site covers:

  • 435 House districts across all 50 states
  • 34 Senate races
  • 39 Governor races
  • Executive Branch — 26 officials including cabinet and senior positions
  • Supreme Court — all 9 justices

You can also browse by state to see which races are active in any state, or browse by branch of government.

Why This Matters

Voters trying to research candidates face a broken information landscape:

  • Editorial sites tell you what to think, filtered through whoever owns the publication
  • Horse-race coverage — the media obsesses over polls and fundraising totals while ignoring policy positions, voting records, and candidate character
  • Raw data sites dump information without helping you make sense of it
  • Social media amplifies noise designed to generate engagement, not understanding

Billions of dollars are spent every cycle to shape what voters believe. Election 2026 is the opposite of that — no ads, no accounts, no tracking, no editorial filter. Just structured questions and sourced answers, powered by the Quarex knowledge system.

Coming Soon: State Proposition Analysis

The next major feature will be state proposition and ballot measure analysis. The same Quarex format that powers candidate profiles will be applied to ballot measures — what a proposition actually does in plain language, arguments for and against, fiscal impact, who supports and opposes it, and historical context.

How Quarex Works

Every candidate profile on this site is a Quarex "Living Book." Each book contains structured chapters organized around specific questions — not free-form text, but a deliberate framework that ensures consistent, comparable coverage across every candidate.

When you open a candidate profile and either use the native Quarex search or paste a question into your preferred AI (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity), the structured data gives the AI the context it needs to answer accurately, with sources, instead of guessing or hallucinating.

Quarex is not an opinion engine. It does not tell you who to vote for. It gives you the information you need to decide for yourself.
